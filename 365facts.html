365 Facts is a quiz app for learning facts on a daily basis


There are 365 days a year!
Wouldn't it be great to use every day to learn a new fact?
This and much more is possible with our 365 Facts app!

Now available in the Play Store!

You will receive a notification every 24 hours with new facts to improve your general knowledge in different Categories.

So that you don't forget this knowledge, you can check the facts in the integrated quiz at any time.

Have fun!
