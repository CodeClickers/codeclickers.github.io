365 Facts is a quiz app for learning facts on a daily basis


There are 365 days a year!
Wouldn't it be great to use every day to learn a new fact?
This and much more is possible with our 365 Facts app!

Now available in the Play Store!

You will receive a notification every 24 hours with new facts to improve your general knowledge in different Categories.

So that you don't forget this knowledge, you can check the facts in the integrated quiz at any time.

Have fun!

Functions:

Check your notifications!

You will receive a notification with a fact every 24 hours!

Press the play button!

The quiz will open and look forward to playing!

Win coins in the quiz!

Coins are won in the quiz by answering questions correctly. Time and number of facts are also taken into account.

Use your coins in the app shop!

In the shop, you can exchange your hard-earned coins for your favorite songs and fonts.

Love your hearts!

The hearts are an important part of our app. If you get a question wrong in the quiz, you lose a heart. All lost hearts reappear after 30 minutes (one heart every 5 minutes).

Look forward!

The upper heart bar shows when the next heart will appear again. You can always buy new hearts in our in-app shop.

Download:

Sadly the app is only available for Android users for now, but we are working on a iOS version!

Now available in the Play Store!
